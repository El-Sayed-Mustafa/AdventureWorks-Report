# Business Intelligence Dashboard Project

This project is a fully interactive Power BI dashboard designed to turn raw business data into meaningful, actionable insights. It demonstrates how real-world business problems can be analyzed, visualized, and interpreted to drive smarter decisions.

**Project Story:**
Imagine a business trying to understand its performance across products, regions, and time. This dashboard answers exactly that—showing not just numbers, but **what the numbers really mean**. From uncovering top-performing products to spotting declining trends, every chart tells a story.

**Key Insights & Highlights:**

* **Top Performers:** Pareto analysis revealed that the top 20% of products generate around **75% of total revenue**, helping prioritize resources.
* **Growth Trends:** Month-over-Month (MoM) sales increased by **12% in Q4**, while Year-over-Year (YoY) revenue growth reached **18%** in key markets.
* **Seasonal Patterns:** Identified peak sales months and slow periods, enabling strategic planning.
* **Customer Behavior:** Insights on purchasing trends and engagement patterns help in targeting promotions effectively.

**Interactive & User-Friendly:**

* Drill-down charts to explore data at a granular level
* Slicers and filters for dynamic exploration
* KPIs that highlight the most critical metrics at a glance

**Data Model & Technical Details:**

* **Relational data model** connecting multiple tables for accurate calculations
* **DAX formulas** for advanced KPIs, trend analysis, and comparisons
* Designed to handle complex business data while remaining intuitive and easy to navigate
